Introducing AI Operators

InTake's AI Operators work your operation the way a senior trade analyst would — only faster, and without ever clocking out. The moment a pedimento, invoice or certificate arrives, they read it, understand it, and put the data to work.

Each Operator is a specialist. SCAN classifies and extracts every document. AUDIT cross-checks it against Mexican customs rules and the new electronic value declaration (MVE), flagging risks before they turn into fines. SCOUT keeps watch over tariffs, permits and suppliers so a bad classification never slips through.

Because they run continuously, nothing waits on a person to be free. The number you need is already calculated, the discrepancy is already caught, and the regulatory change is already on your radar — the moment it happens.

The result is simple: you stop scaling by adding headcount and start scaling by adding intelligence. Your team is freed to decide, while the Operators absorb the repetitive, error-prone work that used to swallow entire days.
