What documents can InTake read?

Pedimentos, facturas, packing lists and certificados de origen — the SCAN agent lifts every field into your operation the moment a PDF arrives, with no manual capture.

How accurate is the compliance validation?

AUDIT runs 122 compliance layers — Ley Aduanera, RGCE, NOMs, T-MEC, PROSEC — on every operation and tells you exactly which article is at risk before the pedimento is filed.

Do I need to change my current workflow?

No. InTake works alongside your existing process — forward your docs by email or upload them, and the agents handle reading, validation and intelligence in the background.

Is my operational data secure?

Yes. Operations are isolated per account and encrypted, credentials and API keys stay server-side, and your data is never shared across clients.

What does the regulatory radar monitor?

The DOF and Banxico monitors track the Diario Oficial and Banco de México in real time, surfacing only the changes that affect the fractions in your operations.
